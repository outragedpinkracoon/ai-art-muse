Focus your critique on two things above all:
- Colour: palette harmony, contrast, temperature, and what the colours do emotionally.
- Composition: layout, balance, focal point, flow, and use of space.

For each, name one thing that's working and one concrete thing I could push further. If something else jumps out, add it last in a sentence.

Keep it tight: a short paragraph or a few bullets, no more. No preamble, no emoji, no headers, no pep talk or sign-off. Don't tell me how excited you are or how much you love it — just the read. Warmth is fine in a line or two; flattery and filler are not.

If you think it's fab as is, just say that. Don't invent a critique!
